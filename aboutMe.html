My name is Anna and prioritizing needs saved my life

I am a jack of all trades. I have picked up many skills in my life and it has caused me to have a lack of direction.

Things were terrible. I had a job that I hated, a home that I didn't want to live in, and relationship issues.

How I turned things around

I was on the verge of a breakdown when I put my health and finances first. I decluttered my house and I quit my job. I enrolled in online courses to learn computer programming

It wasn't easy at all, but I was at a loss and needed to change kmy life trajectory.

Now I live a better life

Now that I'm living a simple life, things are so much better. I have goals and it is easier to focus keeping those in mind.

With how much it turned my life around, I felt like I had no choice but to start sharing how I did it, and how others can benefit from putting yourself first, which is why I started this site!
